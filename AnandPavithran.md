History of Anand Pavithran

Anand Pavithran is an experienced systems architect, educator, and researcher specializing in enterprise Linux infrastructure, cloud-native technologies, and quantum computing. Over a career spanning more than two decades, his path has bridged both industry-standard technical engineering and academic research.

Education & Academic Background

Academic Degrees:
He earned his Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the Government Engineering College, Wayanad (1999–2004), followed by a Master of Engineering (M.Eng) in Computer and Communication Engineering from Mahendra Engineering College (2010–2012).

Teaching & Mentorship:
He has spent extensive time in academia, notably serving as an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science & Engineering department at MES College of Engineering.

Industry Expertise & Technical Background
Alongside his academic tenure, Anand has built a long-standing career as a veteran technical consultant and learning professional:

Enterprise & Cloud Infrastructure:
He is highly proficient in the Red Hat ecosystem, holding advanced credentials including **Red Hat Certified Architect (RHCA) in Infrastructure** and **Red Hat Certified OpenShift Architect**. His core enterprise engineering expertise covers Kubernetes, Ansible Automation Platform, Red Hat OpenShift, cluster management, Ceph storage solutions, and data protection strategies (such as OADP/Velero).

Technical Instruction:
Working as a senior instructor and learning consultant, he has delivered advanced technical training to engineering institutions, industry professionals, and enterprises globally, spanning locations across India, Singapore, Thailand, and Sri Lanka.
